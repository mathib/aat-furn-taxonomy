Abstract

The AAT-FURN taxonomy contains OWL classes to classify **furniture components**. The taxonomy concepts are extracted and restructured from the Getty Art & Architecture Thesaurus ([Getty AAT](https://www.getty.edu/research/tools/vocabularies/aat/about.html)), v2020-07-29. Other taxonomies might define classification concepts for architectural and building system components. The main taxonomy is structured using RDFS subsumption axioms. Beside the main tree structure of classes, an alternative method to navigate the taxonomy is provided based on [gvp:GuideTerm](http://vocab.getty.edu/ontology#GuideTerm) instances connected to the taxonomy classes using [contax:containsGuideTerm](https://w3id.org/con-tax#containsGuideTerm) and [contax:containsConcept](https://w3id.org/con-tax#containsConcept) properties.

The namespace for AAT-FURN terms is https://w3id.org/con-tax/components/aat-furn#

The suggested prefix for the AAT-FURN taxonomy is aat-furn

The Turtle version of the AAT-FURN taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/components/aat-furn.ttl
The RDF/XML version of the AAT-FURN taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/components/aat-furn.rdf
The JSON-LD version of the AAT-FURN taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/components/aat-furn.jsonld
The N-Triples version of the AAT-FURN taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/components/aat-furn.nt

The AAT-FURN taxonomy was created by Mathias Bonduel (KU Leuven) during his PhD research (2017-2020) funded by the Research Foundation Flanders in the form of a Strategic Basic Research grant (grant agreement 1S65917N). Note: Contains information from the J. Paul Getty Trust, Getty Research Institute, the Art & Architecture Thesaurus, which is made available under the ODC Attribution License.
